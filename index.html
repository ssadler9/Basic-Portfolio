My name is Sam Sadler. I am from Henderson, TX. There wasn't a lot to do in Henderson growing up, so I resorted to playing sports and video games! Golf has always been my passion. I have been playing for as long as I can remember, and ended up playing golf for HHS! Following my passion, I went to Mississippi State University to become a Class A PGA Professional.

I eventually reached my goal at Mississippi State and graduated with a Bachelor's in Business and Marketing with a focus in Professional Golf Management. I took my degree and started giving lessons as a Class A professional at Wooodforest Golf Club in Conroe, TX.

After a year of golf, I decided it was time to prepare for my family. I left the industry, and started working in Sales at indeed.com. What an eye-opener that experience has been! I have been able to work with employers to list their open positions online for job seekers to find easily.

Working for a Tech Company lead me down the path I'm on now - becoming a Full Stack Developer. I want to help people get jobs, and the only way I can do that better than I am now is to develop a better code for employers and job seekers to use!
